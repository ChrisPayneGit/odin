Microwave Baked Potato

Description

If you want the yummy taste of a slowly baked potato but aren't patient enough, or don't have the know-how to bake it in an oven, this is for you. Give it 12 minutes and get a mouthwatering, taste bud-tingling treat.

When you're short on time or patience, waiting an hour or more for an oven-baked potato might seem like a tall order. That's why this recipe is so darn good â€“ you can have a perfectly microwaved baked potato in just a little more than 10 minutes! It sounds too good to be true but, we promise, this recipe is just as good as reviewers say it is.

Ingredients

  • Large Russet Potato - 1
  • Salt and Ground Black Pepper
  • Butter - 1 tbsp
  • Shredded Cheedar Cheese - 2 tbsp
  • Sour Cream - 1 tbsp

Steps

  1. Scrub potato and prick with a fork.
  2. Place on a microwave-safe plate. Microwave on full power for 5 minutes. Turn potato over, and microwave until soft, about 5 more minutes.
  3. Remove potato from the microwave, and cut in half lengthwise. Season with salt and pepper and mash up the inside a little with a fork.
  4. Top with sour cream, and serve.
